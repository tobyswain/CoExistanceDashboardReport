Upload source files

Select one or several Excel or CSV exports. Recognised files are applied immediately using controlled replacement rules; overlapping YTD records are not appended twice.

Replacement rules
Unit Count replaces worked activity within its uploaded date coverage. ADP leave replaces leave records within its coverage. Headcount, hours and mapping files merge by employee number or name. Compatible branch-level finance files replace the corresponding baseline measures. The exported workbook is the file to upload to GitHub.

Each source shows what was recognised, what period it covers and whether it was applied.

Accepted source structures

Column names can vary; the dashboard uses common aliases and validates the minimum fields below.

SourceMinimum recognised fieldsDashboard treatment
Unit Count / MovewareDate, name or employee, actual hours; job, customer, role and branch where availableRebuilds worked person-days, jobs, customers, hours, roles and deployment branches
ADP leave / absenceEmployee or name, date or start date; leave or absence typeRebuilds annual leave, other leave and worked/leave conflicts
Headcount / starters / leaversEmployee or name; employee number, branch, role and employment dates where availableUpdates the contracted population and individual profiles
Hours / work patternsEmployee number or name; weekly days, hours or work patternUpdates scheduled capacity and no-deployment calculations
Employee mappingSource or Moveware name; ADP or canonical name and employee numberReconciles unit and HR records before aggregation
Finance / branch baselineBranch plus the existing Baseline Data fields, or branch-level under-utilisation, avoidable, structural and co-existence valuesUpdates financial KPIs without changing activity data
Bank holidaysDate and branch or regionAdjusts scheduled working-day capacity
Customer classificationsCustomer or client and segment, sector or classificationStores governed classifications in the refreshed workbook
Skills / licences / clearancesEmployee and skill, licence, clearance or trainingAdds capability evidence to employee records for future validation
